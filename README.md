# 🎯 Interview Prep Tracker

A clean and minimal topic-by-topic interview preparation tracker built for CS students cracking internships.

🌐 **Live Demo:** [Click Here](https://interview-prep-tracker-sepia.vercel.app)

---

## 💡 The Problem

CS students preparing for placements have no structured way to track topics — DSA, OS, DBMS, CN — what is done, what is pending, what needs revision. Everything is scattered in rough copies or random notes that get lost.

---

## ✨ Features

- 🧠 **Topic Tracker** — Track every important CS topic across 6 subjects
- 📊 **Progress Report** — See your overall readiness score with subject wise breakdown
- ⭐ **Star Important Topics** — Mark topics you need to revise
- ⏱️ **Daily Study Timer** — Set a daily goal and track study hours
- 🔥 **Streak Tracker** — Stay motivated with daily streak counter
- 💬 **Motivational Quotes** — Fresh quote every day to keep you going
- 📱 **Mobile Friendly** — Works on all devices
- 💾 **Auto Save** — Everything saves in browser automatically

---
